Men’s sexual health is more complicated than a single pill can cure.

The problems that can occur are vast and far-reaching. Problems with performance can show up in many different ways, including:

  • Premature ejaculation

  • Inhibited or delayed ejaculation
  • Performance anxiety

Your problems may have started when you were younger. They may be starting to appear as you age. Sexual dysfunctions may be hindering your ability to sustain a physical relationship, or they may be getting in the way of a deeper relationship.
